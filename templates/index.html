About Us

In the era of rapid development of information technology, the ability to recognize human emotions through computer vision has become an important research area and has been widely applied in many fields such as human-computer interaction, security, healthcare and marketing. The ability of computers to automatically recognize and classify human emotions through facial expressions not only opens up many application opportunities but also establishes many technically sophisticated formulas.

Target

This project aims to build an emotion recognition system from facial images. The tasks include collecting and preprocessing image data, developing a machine learning model to classify basic emotions, evaluating and improving the performance of the model. The model will then be deployed to a website to be able to identify emotions through live video.
